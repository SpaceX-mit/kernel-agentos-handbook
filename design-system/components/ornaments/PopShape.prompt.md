Geometric ornament primitive (the "Illustrator layer") — editorial badges, corner marks, dividers. Use sparingly; ornament, not UI chrome.

```jsx
<PopShape name="lozenge" size="md" color="tomato" />
<PopShape name="ring" size="lg" label="03" />
<PopShape name="star" color="coffee" />
```

Names: `circle · ring · dot · square · lozenge · triangle · star · slash`. A `label` centers mono text inside (negative ink on filled shapes).
