Bracketed mono category label that heads every editorial section — use above a title, rule, or feature block.

```jsx
<Kicker jp="目次">CONTENTS</Kicker>
<Kicker tomato jp="特集">FEATURE · 360</Kicker>
```

Brackets are drawn by CSS. Pass `tomato` to tint it the spot colour; `jp` appends a Japanese subtitle after a middot.
