The "ink on paper" loading constellation — four sepia ink-drops that drift (macro), tremble (micro), and pulse their threads (binding rhythm). The brand's signature loader; CSS-only, reduced-motion-gated.

```jsx
<KernelLoading label="LOADING ISSUE 377" />
<KernelLoading size={72} />
```

Use for full-screen splashes and inline waits. `label` adds a mono caption; `size` scales it.
